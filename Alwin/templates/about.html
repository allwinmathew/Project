About Us

We are a team dedicated to helping individuals understand and overcome cognitive biases. Our goal is to make psychological concepts more accessible and provide tools for improving decision-making.

Through our Cognitive Bias Chatbot, we offer a simple way to learn about various biases that affect human thinking and behavior, enabling users to recognize them in everyday life.

Our team consists of psychologists, educators, and tech enthusiasts who aim to make psychology fun and easy to understand!
